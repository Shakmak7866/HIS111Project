Who is Archimedes?

The man who revolutionized math. His findings changed everything that humans will know about the universe. I look up to this man because of how much knowledge there is to be sought from him. I, the humble student, Mathitis will seek to know everything that I can learn from him. For what is the worth of living if not to learn. I forsake everything I have in this world just to learn from one of the greatest mathematicians to ever live.
